Cachly on iPad

Cachly runs on iPad with the same interface as on iPhone, taking advantage of the larger screen — and works alongside other apps with iPadOS multitasking.

iPad

On iPad, Cachly gives you all the same features as on iPhone, with the larger screen giving the map, lists and cache details more room to work. It also supports iPadOS multitasking — Split View and Slide Over — so you can run Cachly beside another app such as Safari or Notes.

🖥
With iCloud sync on, lists and data you set up on iPad are ready on your iPhone in the field.

Everything else is the same

The capabilities described throughout this guide — the map, logging, search, offline maps and more — are all present on iPad, just laid out for the larger display.
