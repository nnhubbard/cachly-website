What’s New

The current version of Cachly is 8.1.6, released February 17, 2026. Here’s what recent releases added, and what’s coming next in Cachly 8.2 and 8.3.

Coming soon

Two releases are in active development:

  • Cachly 8.2 — Cachly Intelligence (AI summaries of logs and descriptions, an AI log assistant, and CarPlay voice summaries) and Multi-User Logging (log a find under several geocaching.com accounts at once). Both are Cachly Pro features.
  • Cachly 8.3 — Track Recording & HealthKit integration.

Cachly 8.1.6 February 17, 2026

A maintenance release with bug fixes:

  • Fixed an issue where some cache images were missing their image names.
  • Restored cache titles in Compass view when viewing live caches.
  • Fixed an issue where trackable images were black and not sending.
  • Fixed an iPad layout issue where the Add Photo button and text counter made the log text area appear too small.

Cachly 8.1 — CarPlay

Cachly 8.1 brought full CarPlay support to Cachly Pro: the complete map (including Pro Offline Maps) on your car’s dashboard, cache lists, navigation to caches, and voice-activated logging with speech-to-text. Read the full announcement: Cachly 8.1 with CarPlay and the follow-up CarPlay update.

Cachly 8.0 — Cachly Pro

Cachly 8.0 introduced Cachly Pro, the optional subscription with worldwide offline vector maps (contours, hillshade, trails, POI, 3D buildings), live cache loading, county and region tracking, the DeLorme Grid, and OSM POI search. Read the full announcement: Cachly 8.0.
