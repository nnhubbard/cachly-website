What Is Cachly?

Cachly is a full-featured geocaching client and an official partner app of Geocaching HQ, built on the official Geocaching.com (Groundspeak) service. It’s designed around an offline-first philosophy: you can download caches, maps and photos ahead of time and find caches with no cell signal, then submit your saved logs from Pending Logs once you’re back online.

Beyond the basics, Cachly offers advanced tools that experienced cachers rely on — coordinate projection, multi-user logging, highlights, filter and text templates, GPX import/export, pocket queries, and deep offline vector maps.

Free vs. Cachly Pro

Cachly is fully usable for everyday geocaching without a subscription. Cachly Pro unlocks premium capabilities such as offline vector maps, DeLorme maps, county tools and advanced live-map features. Learn what’s included on the Cachly Pro page.

Some features also depend on your geocaching.com membership level (basic vs. premium), which is separate from Cachly Pro. See Accounts & Sign In for the distinction.
