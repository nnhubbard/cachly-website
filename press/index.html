About Cachly

Cachly is the most powerful geocaching app for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and CarPlay. Available on the App Store for $4.99 with an optional Pro subscription, Cachly is built by Zed Said Studio LLC as an Authorized Geocaching API Developer, working directly with geocaching.com data. This paragraph is our canonical boilerplate—you are welcome to quote it verbatim in your coverage.

Review copies & contact

Cachly offers press access for coverage: if you are reviewing the app, reach out and we can arrange Pro trial or review access. The best way to contact us is by email at support@cach.ly. To see the community in action, visit @cachlyapp on YouTube and the Cachly Facebook group.

Story angles

Cachly offers several timely angles for coverage of geocaching apps.

  • Geocaching from the dashboard: Cachly Pro brings full geocaching support to CarPlay, including voice-activated logging while driving.
  • On-device AI for geocachers: Cachly Intelligence, coming in version 8.2, uses Apple Intelligence to summarize logs and descriptions, assist with log writing, and read CarPlay voice summaries aloud—running on-device and requiring iPhone 15 Pro or newer.
  • An indie app outrating the big names: Cachly is built by independent developer Nicholas Hubbard of Zed Said Studio LLC in the Pacific Northwest, and holds 4.8 stars from 3,600+ App Store ratings, ranking #7 in Navigation (Paid).
